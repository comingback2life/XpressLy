Frequently Asked Questions

We're committed to providing you with great exchange rates and low fees every single day. To view the current exchange rates and fees, log in to your account and select the country you’d like to send money to.

Transfer varies based on where you’re sending from, where you’re sending to, and your sending limit.

You can cancel most transfers, as long as your recipient hasn't received the funds.

If your transfer hasn't been completed, you can change the method used to deliver money to your recipient. For example, if your recipient can't make it to a cash pickup location, you can change the delivery to their bank account. If you need to update other transfer details, use How to edit your transfer to find out more information about editing an active transfer.

Your credit or debit payment might not work if the card number, expiration data, or CVV or CVC wasn't entered accurately or there isn't enough money in your account to cover the trasnfer. Also, check if your card has expired or hasn't been activated or mayber you reached your credit limit.

If we’re unable to collect your payment, we won’t be able to complete your transfer and you’ll get a notification or alert letting you know what happened. Your money will stay with you.
